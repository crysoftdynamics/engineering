# Architecture Decision Records — Index
 
**Crysoft Dynamics — Sentinel product line & ATLAS Hive runtime**
 
This is the public index of our internal Architecture Decision Records. ADRs capture significant decisions shaping the product, hardware, or runtime architecture — the context, the options considered, the decision, and its consequences. ADRs are immutable once accepted; reversed decisions are superseded by new ADRs rather than edited.
 
Full documents are internal. Titles and statuses are published here as a record of how the system has been reasoned about. Some titles have been generalized where the specific technology choice is competitively sensitive.
